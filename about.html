我们的故事 Our Story

北京市京徽龙江科技有限公司成立于2006年，是一家专注于为生产研发设备与塑料焊接半导体公司，PP、PVDF、PVC、NPP、PE等不同材质的焊接采用不同的焊接方式 ，专业的焊接技术人员，并全部取得了塑料焊接证书。公司10余年生产的设备均无焊接泄漏问题。 Beijing Jinghui Longjiang Technology Co., Ltd. was established in 2006, specializing in production and R&D equipment and plastic welding for semiconductor companies. Different welding methods are used for different materials such as PP, PVDF, PVC, NPP, PE, etc. Professional welding technicians have all obtained plastic welding certificates. The company's equipment produced for more than 10 years has no welding leakage problems.

自成立以来高速发展，现有员工人数近80人，其中40%为技术研发人员，拥有近千平千级车间、万级车间和研发实验室，近3年营收复合增长率超100%。始终坚持创新引领，不断攀登半导体高端湿法装备技术高峰，自主研发的全自动晶圆盒清洗机成功打破国外垄断，并通过国内一线半导体企业验证。 Since its establishment, it has developed rapidly. It now has nearly 80 employees, of which 40% are technical R&D personnel. It has nearly 1,000 square meters of thousand-level workshops, ten-thousand-level workshops and R&D laboratories. The compound annual revenue growth rate in the past 3 years has exceeded 100%. It has always insisted on innovation leadership, constantly climbing the peak of high-end semiconductor wet equipment technology, and the independently developed fully automatic wafer cassette cleaning machine has successfully broken foreign monopoly and passed the verification of domestic first-line semiconductor enterprises.

经过多年的发展，我们已经成为行业内具有影响力的技术、设备服务提供商。 After years of development, we have become an influential technology and equipment service provider in the industry.
